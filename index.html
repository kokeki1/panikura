Slang Terms & Meanings

Welcome to the slang section! Here’s where we decode the wildest words and weirdest phrases.

drip
Someone’s style or fashion game. "Yo, that jacket’s got mad drip."
sus
Short for suspicious. Popularized by Among Us. "He acting sus."
yeet
To throw something with force and confidence. Can also be an exclamation. "YEET!"
rizz
Charisma, especially for flirting. "Dude's got no rizz."
mid
Something average or underwhelming. "That movie was mid."
no cap
“No lie” or “I’m serious.” "That song is fire, no cap."
