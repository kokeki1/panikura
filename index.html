🔤 Panikura Slang Dictionary

If you’ve ever sat in a Panikura convo and thought “What the heck are they saying?”, you’re in the right place.

blorbified
Emotionally attached to something or someone in a way that defies logic. “I just met this bug in a game and I’m already blorbified.”
egg mode
A state of pure confusion, uselessness, or just being a silly little guy. “I went full egg mode during that meeting.”
panikura
Used as a verb, noun, or general state of being. Meaning depends on context. Example: “She panikura’d out of the VC.”
grumbo
Insult or affectionate nickname depending on tone. "Shut up, you little grumbo."
brain goo
Mental state when nothing computes. “Homework made my brain goo.”
clown-certified
Officially embarrassing, but in a way the group respects. “He admitted he sent the meme to his professor. Clown-certified.”
emoji speak
Talking entirely in emojis or mimicking them with real words. “He hit me with the ‘skull emoji IRL’ and I died.”
